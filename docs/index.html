Dreaming of having your own wardrobe consultant? Not sure what the weather is going to do with what your wearing? We have the perfect solution…. Virtual Closet! It’s easy, it’s fun and it’s uniquely YOU!

Friends in high places

Virtual Closet allows users to store images of their clothes in the cloud for easy perusal. The application leverages local weather forecasts and returns appropriate clothing combinations to the users phone. Users can plan ahead and send themselves a outfit ahead of time, or just browse through the clothes in their closet before they even get up.

It's beautiful

Rain or shine, red or blue. Watch the demo and learn how it can work for you. See how easy it is to match your daily outfit with the weather, and see how easy it is to be beautifully stylish!

Easy to integrate

Upload images of your clothing, shoes and accessories. This creates your own virtual closet. Once you have it, you can add or delete as you go.

Features

Organize

Jeans, sport coat, or formal? Not a problem. The app organizes your garments and accessories by subject and style.

Discover

Using our Virtual Assistant, arrange the right combination of clothing and shoes, and weather appropriate wear. We have you covered, in rain or shine!

Plan

What to wear is easy, plan your selection with color and weather appropriate combinations. White for hot weather, and warm reds for cold days. Use our app to stay warm, be cool, and always look hot!
